Dragon Data Usage License V1

Section 1) Definitions

1.1) Model Parameters - Internal variables in the Artificial Intelligence model which is adjusted during the training process to give a specific output via the input of data.

1.2) Prior Intimation Notice - A formal notice sent to the copyright holder(s) stating intended use which includes a formal permission request that can be submitted as evidence in a court of law in case of a dispute. It must be in either digital or physical form.

1.3) Copyright Fair Use Permission - A permission of usage given by the copyright holder(s) allowing the use of their intellectual property(IP) for the stated use as requested in the prior intimation notice as defined in Section 1.2 of this license.

1.4) Scraping - The unauthorized extraction of data without abiding by the contract set by the copyright holder(s).

1.5) Inference - The process of using an Artificial Intelligence model to provide output.

1.6) Parameter Ownership - The partial ownership of the specific model parameters that use data governed by this license and are derivative works of the copyrighted data in the model parameters.

1.7) Royalties - Payments made to the copyright holder(s) for the use of their intellectual property as dictated by the copyright holder(s).

1.8) Model Disgorgement - The deletion or destruction of model parameters of the Artificial Intelligence model.

1.9) SaaS - Software as a Service

Section 2) Data Governance

2.1) Data use - Data provided in the public domain protected by intellectual property legislation being used for derivative works such as Artificial Intelligence models require a prior intimation notice and a copyright fair use permission from the copyright holder(s) before the data can be used for its intended purpose.

2.2) Model Parameter Ownership - The copyright holder(s) reserve all the rights to assert partial ownership of the model parameters trained with the governed data and the right to a share of the earnings made through the derivative works in the form of royalties.

2.3) Jurisdictional Protection - The copyright holder(s) reserve the right to protect their works in the jurisdiction(s) in which the intellectual property is registered in.

2.4) Royalty per Inference - If an Artificial Intelligence model is offered under SaaS or any other commercial model where the model parameters are not publicly available, the copyright holder(s) reserve the right to charge royalty per inference for the specific model parameters under their partial ownership.

2.5) Exceptions - The copyright holder(s) reserves the right to make exceptions in section 2) if required.

Section 3) Breach of License

3.1) Breach resolution - Violations of sections 2.1 or 2.2 grant copyright holder(s) the right to seek interim injunction against the commercial distribution and services enabled through the use of the derivative works by the violating legal entities should any such violation be found by the copyright holder(s). Data extraction without a prior intimation notice and a copyright fair use permission will be deemed as scraping.

3.2) Model Disgorgement - Copyright holder(s) have the right to request for model disgorgement if a breach of license is found and the governed data used to train an Artificial Intelligence model illegally.
