Viewing Protected Content Contract

Version 1.0

Section 1) Definitions

1.1) DDUL-V1 - Dragon Data Usage License Version 1

1.2) IP - Intellectual Property

1.3) Piracy - The unauthorized distribution of copyrighted content which includes content under IP protection.

Section 2) Contract Governance

2.1) Contractual Agreement - By viewing the content either through electronic or non electronic means, you agree to the terms of this unilateral contract.

2.2) Data Governance - All data under this contract is governed by DDUL-V1 license and any breach of the license will be strictly dealt with as per the provisions under DDUL-V1.

2.3) IP holders - The IP holder(s) are {{ legal_entities }}. All rights are reserved to make exceptions and restrictions under the terms of this contract by the IP holders.

2.4) Jurisdiction - All disputes will be subject to {{ primary_jurisdiction }} jurisdiction and may be extended to other jurisdictions in which the IP is registered in.

2.5) Content Distribution Governance - Except for the IP holder(s) or anyone authorized under the provisions of DDUL-V1 any form of piracy or unauthorized use of the content under the governance of this contract is strictly prohibited.

Section 3) Breach of Contract

3.1) Breach Resolution - All breaches will be dealt with under the provisions of DDUL-V1.
