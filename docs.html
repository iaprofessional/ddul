How to embed DDUL or VPCC??? A quick start guide

DDUL(Dragon Data Usage License)

To get started with embedding the DDUL license in a website or an app's Terms of Service add the following line:-

All data usage which is intended for the purpose of use by artificial intelligence or similar technologies is governed by the DDUL-V1 license on this website. Any breach of DDUL-V1 will be dealt with as per the definitions, usage policies and penalties which includes the revocation of usage of this service as defined in said license. Any form of piracy or illegal commercial activities enabled by the use of data governed by DDUL-V1 will be dealt with as per applicable laws and the provisions of DDUL-V1.

Note that you are allowed to add your own set of restrictions or exceptions as per the provisions of the DDUL license.

VPCC(Viewing Protected Content Contract)

Step 1: Open the VPCC creator.

Step 2: Enter required details and download HTML or Markdown file.

Step 3: Insert the required details(in the form of HTML, Markdown or plain text) in copyright information section.

Step 4: OPTIONAL: Include an invisible signature that spells DDUL or VPCC along with version number.

While an invisible signature for creative works has not been standardized yet - it is strongly recommended to add it in order to prevent arguments in court about no knowledge about the contract and license.
